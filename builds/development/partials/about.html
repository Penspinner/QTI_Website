Keeping you up to speed with technology

Quarterhorse Technology Incorporated (QTI) was founded in 1999 as a systems integrator. The vision was to create a bridge that would connect the needs of companies with solutions made available by vendors.  Throughout our history, we have cemented a close relationship with vendors through certifications and long term partnerships. These relationships allowed QTI to understand the needs of the customers and present solutions with leading edge technology to fit clients’ business plans. Hardware and software longevity and solutions that complete all opportunities and cost, are factors that helped develop the relationship.

QTI believes that work should not be limited to a single destination. QTI both embraced and provided new technology solutions that allowed for a combination of regional and remote workforce. Our main office in NYC is next to Penn Station, which staffed dispatchers and onsite engineers. Adhering to our philosophy, we have enhanced our team with talented staff in Pennsylvania, Wisconsin, and Florida.

In addition to main office in NYC, all hardware and datacenter services are strategically located in East and West Coast Datacenters.
